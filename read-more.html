About This Memorial Stone

Heart in beautiful blue granite from southern India. The edge is half polished and half beaten. Natural stone with a shiny polished surface. Beautiful natural stone with a slightly masculine expression.

Material & Origin

This type of granite is an overseas deep rock that was formed far below the earth's crust. This has given it a very solid structure, which makes it particularly suitable for the Danish climate.

This blue-gray surface of the granite gives a calm expression, which comes to life and is experienced with a more vibrant structure when the stone gets wet.

Natural Variations

It should be noted that all natural stones are different, and it is therefore expected that the grain patterns and structures may vary from stone to stone.

All of our urns undergo quality control in terms of surface, structure and appearance, ensuring you receive a memorial of the highest standard.
