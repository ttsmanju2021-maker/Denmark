Father-Mother Stone

Mother/father stones are also called name stones, and are a beautiful and loving way to add an extra memory from you as a daughter or son to the grave. Here on the page you can see a selection of granite types and stones and design your own memorial stone for your father or mother. We will then engrave the stone, deliver it and set it up for you.​

How much does a mother/father tombstone cost?

We offer high quality gravestones for both mom and dad at very reasonable prices. A mom stone or dad stone for the grave costs DKK 3,450, including engraving, delivery and professional installation.
