The Beginnings of GRANIT WORLD

          Our generational family history makes Granit deeply connected to the families and communities we serve, offering a legacy of skilled hands and quiet pride in every memorial we create. Our promise has been simple from the beginning: every life deserves to be remembered with care, dignity, and meaning. More than seventy years on, that promise still holds. The tools have changed. The scale has grown. But the heart behind our work remains steady, respectful, and deeply human. Whether it’s a headstone for someone dearly loved or a memorial made for many, we begin by listening, and finish only when the quality speaks for itself.

Memorial Stone Mason: Tradition and Trust

          Our Sydney-based facility brings together the heritage of traditional stone masonry with the precision of modern technology. From wet-polished finishes and shaped granite to sandblasted inscriptions, each memorial is crafted using detailed design, digital profiling, and one of Australia’s largest 5-axis stone CNC machine. We source our products, and manufacture locally and globally. All installations comply with all relevant Australian Standards, and our in-house water/stone waste recycling plant reduces waste and minimises environmental impact. At Globe Memorial, we honour the old ways and embrace the new, creating memorials that are personal, considered, and made to stand the test of time. We are proud and active members of the Monumental Masons’ Association of NSW (MMA), a non-profit body of independent Master Monumental Masons dedicated to upholding excellence, safety, and industry standards across New South Wales. The MMA provides essential support to both members and cemetery operators, assisting in interpreting and implementing Australian Standards for Cemeteries. It is also consultative in TAFE training requirements and cemetery policy development. Our director, Glenn, currently serves as President of the Association and, alongside Peter Darroch, has contributed to reviewing and updating national standards, reflecting our ongoing commitment to quality, compliance, and leadership within the industry.

Support You Can Trust

          We understand that choosing a memorial is deeply personal, and often unfamiliar. That’s why we take the time to walk alongside you, not just through the process, but through whatever you bring to it. Some come with a clear vision. Others need guidance, reassurance, or simply space to talk things through. Wherever you begin, we’re here to help with care, experience, and empathy. We offer free quotes, site consultations, and thoughtful support from the first meeting to the final installation. Whether your needs are simple or complex, we meet you with openness and understanding and help shape a tribute that feels right.
