Buying a lasting memorial for your loved ones is a matter of trust. At Jelling Stenhuggeri & Online Gravsten, we understand this very well. It is our desire to make it uncomplicated, cheap and safe to buy a tombstone or urn online.
By purchasing a stone online from us, you get the opportunity to sit in your own, quiet surroundings, alone or surrounded by family. You get plenty of time to choose and design the perfect stone to commemorate, and we are always available and happy to guide you safely through the entire process from advice to ordering to delivery and installation of the stone in the cemetery.



Buy a beautiful gravestone, urn or lawn stone from us - we give you a price guarantee

At Jelling Stonemasonry & Online Gravestone, we have made it easier and cheaper for you to order gravestones, urns and lawn stones, without compromising on quality, craftsmanship or security. Here you have time to choose and design your stone at your leisure, you can follow the price all the way - so there are no unpleasant surprises.
